About Us

Empowering Research Excellence Since 2024

Founded in 2024, SARG Research Biolabs functions as a research division of the Institute for Applied Research and Training, in collaboration with GANESH Scientific Research Foundation (ISO 9001:2015 Certified Research and Testing Laboratory).

We foster a dynamic ecosystem that empowers scholars, institutions, and research organizations through research, training, collaboration, and innovation-driven initiatives.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center for research and training that fosters innovation, ethical scholarship, scientific excellence, and sustainable solutions to complex global challenges.

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality research training aligned with international standards
  • Facilitate cutting-edge, multidisciplinary research
  • Build collaborative ecosystems connecting researchers worldwide
  • Uphold the highest standards of ethics and academic integrity
